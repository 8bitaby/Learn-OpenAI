

Recipe Title: Spicy Chicken Fried Rice

Ingredients:
- 2 cups cooked white rice
- 1 small onion, diced
- 1 large tomato, diced
- 1 small red chilli, finely chopped
- 1 cup cooked chicken, diced

Instructions:
1. Heat a medium-sized skillet over medium heat.
2. Add the diced onion and cook until softened, about 2-3 minutes.
3. Add the diced tomato and chilli, and cook for an additional minute.
4. Add the cooked chicken and cook until heated through, about 3-4 minutes.
5. Add the cooked rice, stirring to combine.
6. Continue to cook, stirring occasionally, until the rice is hot and all ingredients are fully incorporated.
7. Serve hot. Enjoy!
