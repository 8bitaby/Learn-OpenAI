

Q1. What is the capital of India?
A. New Delhi
B. Mumbai
C. Bangalore

Correct Answer: A. New Delhi


Q2. What is the official language of India?
A. Hindi
B. Urdu
C. English

Correct Answer: A. Hindi


Q3. Who is the current Prime Minister of India?
A. Narendra Modi
B. Rahul Gandhi
C. Arvind Kejriwal

Correct Answer: A. Narendra Modi


Q4. What is the currency of India?
A. Rupee
B. Dollar
C. Pound

Correct Answer: A. Rupee


Q5. What is the official religion of India?
A. Hinduism
B. Buddhism
C. Islam

Correct Answer: A. Hinduism
